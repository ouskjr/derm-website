How a visit works

Three small steps.

i.

You write or call

WhatsApp is fastest. Tell me what's going on with your skin, when you'd like me, and where in NJ or NY you are.

ii.

I bring everything

Table, towels, products, music if you want it. Clear a corner of your living room — that's the only prep on your end.

iii.

You go straight to bed

No traffic home, no parking, no waiting. Skin set, lights low, the rest of the evening already saved for you.

The best skin work happens slowly, in your own light, with no one waiting on the next chair.
