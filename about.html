My story

Skincare is personal.
So is my approach.

I'm Magdana Machavariani, a Licensed Esthetician based in Brielle, New Jersey. With years of professional training and hands-on experience, I specialize in personalized skincare treatments that deliver real, visible results.

I founded Lamazi with one belief: great skincare shouldn't require you to leave home. Every client deserves a treatment that fits their schedule, their skin, and their life. That's why I come to you — fully equipped, fully prepared, and fully focused on your skin.

Whether you're looking to address a specific concern or simply want to invest in your skin's health, I'll create a treatment plan that works for you.
