How a visit works.

i.

Send a short note

Tell me what's going on with your skin, where you are along the shore, and a few times that work. The quickest way is WhatsApp; the contact form works too.

ii.

I write back the same day

You'll get two or three openings to choose from. We'll lock in a date, a treatment, and your address — that's the whole confirmation.

iii.

I bring everything

Table, towels, products, music if you want it. Clear a corner of your living room — that's the only prep on your end.

iv.

You stay home afterwards

No drive, no parking, no waiting room. The treatment ends — you go straight to bed with skin set and a small note for next time.
